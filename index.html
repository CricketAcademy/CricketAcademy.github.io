Cricket is the second most popular sport in the world. Originating in England, cricket now has spread all over the world, particularly influencing the subcontinent and Australia. Since the introduction of limited overs cricket, bowlers require a large arsenal of deliveries and batsman, an equal variety of shots. This website will teach you all the basics needed to survive in modern day cricket, as well as information I have received throughout my own journey.
