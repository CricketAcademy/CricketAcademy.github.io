I am a 15 year old cricket enthusiast who has played and watched the game, since I was 5. I started club cricket at the age of 9, and soon developed a passion for it. I practiced and practiced, usually by myself, but that didn't faze me. I researched tips online and tried to implement them in practice, whether it was a new shot or how to bowl faster. I didn't have access to good coaching or books to learn from. However, thanks to the online tips I was able to find and practice, I improved and made it to div 1. My years of online research are what inspired me to create this website. Since making it to div 1 and trialling for the ACT comets team, I have picked up lots of information from a variety of different cricketers. This website is a compilation of the information which I have gathered throughout my journey.
